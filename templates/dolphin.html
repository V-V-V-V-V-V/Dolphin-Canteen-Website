DOLPHIN CANTEEN

Dolphin Canteen is the main canteen of the Magis block. Breakfast, lunch and snacks are all viable at all times, quick and quality are our mottos. The served is healthy fresh and utmost important to hygiene is given.

AboutUs

Why Choose us?

We are a big part of St. Joseph University. Our services are not just limited to students but teachers but also to staff and visitors. People love our food and we ensure that our standards are up to mark. Student demands are given the highest priority with constant changes being made based on user feedback. We also provide mid day meals for the lesser privileged. Our services wont disappoint you.
